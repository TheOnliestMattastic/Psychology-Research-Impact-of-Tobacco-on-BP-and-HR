Impact of Tobacco on Blood Pressure & Heart Rate

Undergraduate Research Project by Matthew Poole Chicano

Overview

This project explores the relationship between tobacco use and cardiovascular health indicators (blood pressure and heart rate). It demonstrates reproducible data analysis in R, combining data cleaning, descriptive statistics, ANOVA, and visualization.
